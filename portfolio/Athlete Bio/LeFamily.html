Family

LeBron James, often referred to as "King James," has a close-knit family known as the "James Gang." His family consists of five members:

  • Savannah James: His wife, whom he met in high school. She is a businesswoman and philanthropist.
  • Bronny James: Their eldest son, born in 2004, who has followed in his father's footsteps and is now an NBA player.
  • Bryce James: The middle child, born in 2007, also a promising basketball player.
  • Zhuri James: The youngest, born in 2014, who has gained popularity through social media and family appearances.
